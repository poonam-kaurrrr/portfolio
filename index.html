ABOUT ME

Hey! I’m Poonam Kaur, a 19-year-old UI/UX designer who’s passionate about clean, minimalistic design. I believe less really is more — my goal is to create interfaces that are simple, intuitive, and easy to use without any extra clutter. I focus on clear layouts, soft color palettes, and purposeful spacing to keep things feeling light yet functional. I’m eager to learn new tools and techniques to improve user experience and bring fresh ideas.
